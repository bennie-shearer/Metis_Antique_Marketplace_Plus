# Metis Antique Marketplace Plus -- Support Resources

**Version 1.2.48**

External organizations and resources for antique dealers, collectors,
appraisers, and shop operators.

---

## Professional appraisal organizations

**American Society of Appraisers (ASA)**
Largest multi-discipline appraisal organization. Certified members searchable by specialty.
https://www.appraisers.org

**Appraisers Association of America (AAA)**
Specialists in fine and decorative arts. Members listed by category and region.
https://www.appraisersassociation.org

**International Society of Appraisers (ISA)**
Residential contents and personal property specialists. Online directory.
https://www.isa-appraisers.org

**Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP)**
Required reading for appraisals used for insurance, estate, or charitable donation.
https://www.appraisalfoundation.org/uspap

## Selling platforms

**1stDibs** -- High-end antiques and luxury items. Dealer application required.
https://www.1stdibs.com/dealers/

**Chairish** -- Mid-century modern, vintage, and antique furniture and decor.
https://www.chairish.com/sell

**Ruby Lane** -- Antiques and collectibles marketplace. Monthly shop fees apply.
https://www.rubylane.com/selling

**eBay** -- Broad audience. Strong for collectibles, estate jewelry, ephemera.
https://www.ebay.com/sl/sell

**Etsy** -- Vintage (20+ years) and handmade items. Large buyer base for smaller pieces.
https://www.etsy.com/sell

**LiveAuctioneers** -- Online bidding platform for traditional auction houses.
https://www.liveauctioneers.com/partner/

**Invaluable** -- Auction aggregator connecting houses with online bidders.
https://www.invaluable.com/auction-houses/

https://www.brimfieldshow.com

**Round Top Antiques Week** -- Texas. Spring and fall. Major mid-century and Americana.
https://www.roundtoptexasantiques.com

**Scott Antique Markets** -- Atlanta and Columbus OH. Monthly.

## Price research resources

**WorthPoint** -- Subscription database of sold prices from major platforms.
https://www.worthpoint.com

**Kovels** -- Antiques and collectibles price guide. Books and online subscription.
https://www.kovels.com

**Terapeak (via eBay)** -- Sold price research for eBay listings. Free with seller account.
https://www.ebay.com/sh/research

**Invaluable Price Results** -- Searchable auction results database.
https://www.invaluable.com/price-results/

**Miller's Antiques Handbook & Price Guide** -- Annual UK/international reference.
